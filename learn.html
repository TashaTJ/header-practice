Make Your Own...

You don't need to be Picasso to create your own Pawsome Pawtrait! If you have Procreate installed on a tablet, and an electronic pencil, follow these 9 easy steps to make your own!

  • 1. Find a Perfect Pooch Picture & import to procreate
  • 2. Trace the out line of the dog
  • 3. Add the largest block colour
  • 4. Add the second largets block colour
  • 5. Add in some smaller detail, like hair
  • 6. Add some in shading
  • 7. Combine all the previous layers and add any final details
  • 8. Remove the background
  • 9. Add a circular background disk, in a vibrant colour
